Angel's Recipe for the Best Cinanon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yoke
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

    • For the Filling:

    • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
    • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinanon
    • 1/4 cup water

    Instruction:

    1. Mix the milk with yeas, sugar and eggs
    2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
    4. Knead the tough for 10minutes
    5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with a plastic wrap, leave it somewhere to rise for 2hrs.
    6. After the dough
